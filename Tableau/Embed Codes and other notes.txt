I finally realized tonight that using formulas in different Excel worksheets to aggregate the data was so much faster and easier than fighting with Tableau to ignore the columns I don’t currently care about.  I’ve committed the spreadsheet and the *.twbx files to GitHub.

I changed non-Maine donations to “From Away” because I couldn’t initially figure out how to do a manual sort in Tableau on the Region category.  After I figured it out (it turns out to be simple), I decided to let you guys decide whether it was insulting or too flippant.  Being a From Awayer myself, I don’t always know what is acceptable.
